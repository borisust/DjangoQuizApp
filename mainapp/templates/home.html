QUIZ APP

Host quizzes in three easy steps:

Register an account

Create an account for free by simply choosing a username and a password. An account is required to create quizzes, but anyone can view public quizzes and submit responses without registration.

Click here to register

Create a quiz

The quiz creator provides an intuitive interface for composing and customizing quiz content. Set a title, add questions, and specify answer options for your quiz. Progress is saved in your profile, so you can leave and resume quiz creation at any point.

Click here to create a new quiz

Publish your quiz

Set the accessibility of your quiz and publish it. You may choose to restrict access to your quiz with a password or make it public and visible to everyone on the explore page. You can change quiz access settings or stop publishing at any point from your profile. From there you can also access other users' responses to your quizzes.
